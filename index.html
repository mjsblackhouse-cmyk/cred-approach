Michael Smith AO

My interest lies in helping the leaders, and their teams, of purposeful organisations deal with their most consequential, complex, culture-shaping decisions.

I work with boards and executive teams by being present at the table during critical decisions. Leaders develop capabilities whilst making real decisions on real challenges, not through workshops or coaching sessions removed from operational context.

What I Do

Through Black House, I provide strategic support to boards and leadership teams facing complex challenges. This typically includes:

  • Board effectiveness reviews and development programmes
  • Executive team performance and culture work
  • Strategic guidance during periods of significant change or complexity
  • Independent chair or director roles during critical periods

My approach involves sustained engagement rather than project-based consulting. Most client relationships span several years, allowing for development through multiple decision cycles.

Who I Work With

I typically work with:

  • Boards and chairs facing complex governance challenges
  • CEOs and executive teams navigating significant transitions
  • Organisations requiring independent expertise during critical periods
  • Boards seeking to lift their collective effectiveness

Client organisations have ranged from ASX-listed companies to government entities, from large not-for-profits to private enterprises across multiple sectors.

Approach

Board work is a team activity. Directors decide collectively and need to function as high-performing teams, adapting processes and judgement to the matters before them.

My work focuses on helping boards and leadership teams reach their potential as guardians and leaders of their organisations. This is less about scoring past performance and more about identifying the next stage of evolution and collaborating on plans that can be successfully implemented.

The work is evidence-based, using relevant benchmarks and established instruments to compare purpose, principles, practices and performance against comparable organisations.
